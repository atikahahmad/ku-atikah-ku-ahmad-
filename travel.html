Thailand

The first thing before entering Thailand is queuing for a border stamp or passport at the immigration counter.


Second, we stopped by to exchange money


Third, we stopped at 7-Elevent. Mandatory place for Malaysians to come to Thailand, various products available there that are not available at 7-Elevent Malaysia.


Fourth, we arrived in Padang Besar, Thailand.


Here is a food paradise. A variety of food is available there for sale at reasonable prices.


Last stop, we stopped at the famous Kuey Teow Siamese soup shop there it very delicious.
